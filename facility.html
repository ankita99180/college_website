Our Facilities

Our facilities

Institute has medical facility and has all the required first-aid medicines to aid the needy. The Institute has a tie up with Agrawal Orthopedic Hospital for providing medical facilities to all the staff and students. Ambulance service is also available that can be availed in case of emergency.
BGI has a fully air-conditioned conference hall having state-of-the-art facilities.
In the campus there are 3 spacious and air conditioned seminar halls equipped with audio-video aids. This seminar hall serves the purpose of conducting seminar, presentation and workshops for cocurricular, extra-curricular and placement activities.

Library

Institute provides the facility of e-library with the high- speed internet access and variety of e-journals, to the students. Institute also provides Open access facility to students. Library includes 23489 books and 2624 titles.

Cafateria

Institute has extended the two canteen facilities to its students and staff which are located within the campus. Refreshments are available at the college canteen at affordable rates.

Classrooms

Our classrooms are well ventilated, spacious and equipped with overhead and LCD projectors. The furniture used in classrooms is of un-compromised quality to enable students for 6 hours of comfortable and continuous learning.
